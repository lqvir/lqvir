Research Interests

My research interests lie at Secure Multiparty Computation and its applications. I am particularly fascinated by Private Set Intersection and Function Secret Sharing, and my work aims to contribute valuable insights to the ongoing discourse in these areas.

I am currently engaged in the study of Post-Quantum Cryptography, with a keen interest in exploring its potential implications in the field of Post-Quantum Secure Multi-Party Computation (SMPC).
